About Summer World

Independent thinking.
Grounded in practice.

Summer World is a boutique management consultancy built on a simple conviction: sustainable performance improvement comes from understanding how work actually flows through your organisation — and then redesigning it, thoughtfully, with the people closest to it.

With experience across manufacturing, logistics, financial services, and professional services, I bring a rigorous analytical lens and a deeply pragmatic approach to every engagement. No off-the-shelf frameworks. No junior analysts learning on your dime. Just focused, senior-level expertise applied to your most pressing operational challenges.

What I Do

Practical expertise across your most critical operational challenges

01

Process Redesign

End-to-end mapping, analysis, and reimagining of core business processes. I identify the friction, waste, and bottlenecks eroding your performance — then build leaner, more resilient flows in their place.

02

Operational Excellence

Installing the management systems, governance rhythms, and continuous improvement culture that turn one-off projects into permanent performance gains. Results that outlast the engagement.

03

Supply Chain Optimisation

Strategic review and redesign of your supply chain — from procurement and inventory strategy to distribution network design and supplier relationship management — to reduce cost and improve reliability.

04

Performance Transformation

Translating operational improvements into measurable financial and strategic outcomes. I connect the operational and the commercial, ensuring your investment in improvement delivers to the bottom line.

The Approach

How I take you from here to there

01

Discover

Deep operational diagnostics — observing, interviewing, and measuring — to understand how your organisation really works, not just how it appears on paper.

02

Diagnose

Rigorous root-cause analysis to surface the true constraints and identify the highest-leverage opportunities — not just the most obvious ones.

03

Design

Co-creating solutions with your team. The people closest to the work shape the answer — which is why they'll actually implement it.

04

Deploy

Hands-on support through implementation and stabilisation, with a clear plan for embedding the change so performance gains become permanent.
